Photography

Photography

Photography selections for the Economic Security Project must be authentic and human, with clear subjects treated with dignity. Points of guidance, as well as examples below:

  • DO NOT use photos to represent ESP's broader ideas literally (ex: an image of people reaching for cash to convey "guaranteed income", stock photo of a bag of coins to denote "economic security"). This overly simplifies the organization's work.
  • DO utilize collages (in Collage Illustrations section) to build ideas with visual nuance.
  • DO use photos to show people in strong, empowered settings—not despondent, without hope. The ESP brand aims to visualize the power of human movements and dynamic ideas.
  • DO frame photographs within a circular shape with a flattened base to provide an anchor, OR place photographs in full bleed.
