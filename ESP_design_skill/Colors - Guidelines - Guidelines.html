Colors

Colors

The Economic Security Project's C3 and C4 share the same color palette, with different points of emphasis:

  • The C3 is differentiated by the Soft Green as its main brand color (used in the waves logo), while
  • The C4 relies on the Warm Red (used in the spark logo).

Both arms of the organization are anchored by the Warm Navy and Beige. The Secondary Palette (the Deep Purple, Warm Pink, and Warm Gold), are used for supporting elements such as collage backgrounds.

        Color Accessibility

        Refer to the color accessibility chart below to ensure all text on brand materials are legible and clear (ADA compliant). When designing branded materials, reference the accessibility chart below to ensure ADA compliance.

        • 🟩 All color combinations with a green check mark pass accessibility (the higher the number beside it, the higher recommendation for use).
        • 🟨 Color combinations with a yellow check should only be used for large text OR supporting elements such as visual patterns and connection arcs.
        • 🟥 Color combinations with a red X do NOT pass ADA compliance and should not be used.
